Class Detail

vxlRenderer(vw)
One of the main classes of Voxelent's Nucleo A renderer object encapsulates most of the low level calls to WebGL. It is here where Nucleo obtains a reference to the WebGL context. It is also here here (and in the Actor class) where all the attributes and uniforms are passed to the rendering program.
Author: Diego Cantor.
